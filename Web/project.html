World Health Organization

The World Health Organization (WHO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health. Established on April 7, 1948, its mission is to promote health, keep the world safe, and serve the vulnerable. Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, WHO works with 194 member states to address global health challenges, including disease prevention, health emergencies, and the promotion of universal health coverage. It plays a crucial role in coordinating responses to global health crises, setting standards for healthcare, and providing guidance on health-related policies and practices.

Recall and Precision Calculations:

Participants:

  • Participant 1: Electrical Engineering student at Afeka College, knowledgeable in films.

Assumptions: Since it is not possible to objectively know the number of relevant results, we will use heuristics:
First, we will define the best directors as directors who have won the Oscar.
We found that there are about 75 Academy Award-winning directors, and the average number of films per winning director is about 25.
So, let's say there are about 1875 movies that meet the requirement.
The Rotten Tomatoes website has about 150,000 movies, of which we reviewed only 10,000.
So, let's say we reviewed about 125 films that meet the criteria of a film belonging to a successful director.

Calculations:

  • Precision (P1): 7/10 = 0.7
  • Recall (P1): 7/125 = 0.056
  • Precision (P2): 7/10 = 0.7
  • Recall (P2): 7/125 = 0.056


The improvement :

After analyzing the users' score data, we found that they chose relevant movies as movies where the actor Robert De Niro plays there,
so we would like the next query to display more relevant data for those users.
A query that can improve the results:

"Find movies starring actor Robert De Niro".
